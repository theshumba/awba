FAQ

Questions, answered.

Isn’t this just another prayer app?

No. Reminder apps tell you it’s time. Awba is about why it’s worth it — each practice tied to the hadith, the story, and the Name of Allah behind it.

What if I’ve been away for a while?

Then Awba was built for you. We reward the return and never mark the absence — no streaks, no scores, no guilt mechanics. Iman has seasons.

How much time does it take?

About five calm minutes. Understanding the why shouldn’t need a two-hour lecture.
